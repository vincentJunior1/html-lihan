HOW TO WITHDRAW MONEY IN TAIWAN WITH AN INDONESIAN DEBIT CARD

All ATM Cards that have MasterCard, Cirrus, and Visa can be used to withdraw money at Taiwan ATMs. The maximum withdrawal in Taiwan varies, depending on the limit of each card used. The maximum withdrawal for one day is NTD19,000.

HOW TO WITHDRAW MONEY IN TAIWAN WITH AN INDONESIAN DEBIT CARD

1. Make sure the ATM machine has a VISA/ MASTERCARD/ CIRRUS logo like the logo above.

2. Make sure your ATM card has VISA / MASTERCARD / CIRRUS.

3. Make withdrawals like ATMs in general in Indonesia.

4. Usually every ATM has the option to select language, Chinese, English, and other languages!

5. The fee per transaction is IDR25,000 per withdrawal, and admin fee per transaction is 100NTD
