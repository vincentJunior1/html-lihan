About Us
As a foreign student, it is quite difficult to explore Taiwan because of language boundaries, unfamiliarity with the culture and other stuff. We have spent three years in Taiwan figuring out, and discovering new things, getting closer to their culture and learning more about Taiwan. We have decided to create a project called GIFT, giving you tips and tricks for Indonesians who want to come for settle in Taiwan as foreigners easily.
